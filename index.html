who are we?

Nestled in the heart of a the blissful Tigoni region, the Elegido Restaurant offers a cozy retreat for those seeking a culinary experience infused with warmth and charm. Established by Elegido Leonardo together with Chef Ramirez in 2015, this beloved eatery has become a cherished gem within the community. It offers rustic yet elegant décor, adorned with vintage wine barrels and flickering modern and vintage lighting. The Elegido Restaurant invites patrons to savor not just delectable cuisine, but an ambiance that feels like home. Chef Ramirez's menu reflects her passion for seasonal, locally sourced ingredients, with dishes ranging from comforting classics to inventive culinary delights. Whether it's a leisurely brunch with friends or a romantic dinner for two, each visit to the Elegido Restaurant promises an unforgettable journey for the senses.

At Elegido Restaurant, culinary delight awaits with a diverse array of dishes that celebrate the bounty of the seasons. Infused with creativity and passion, the menu reflects a commitment to showcasing the freshest local ingredients, sourced directly from nearby farms and producers. From farm-to-table salads bursting with vibrant colors and flavors to hearty mains that pay homage to traditional recipes with a modern twist, each dish is a masterpiece of culinary craftsmanship. Whether indulging in the delicate flavors of a seafood risotto or savoring the rich aromas of slow-roasted meats, every bite at Elegido Restaurant is a celebration of quality and authenticity. With options to suit every palate, including vegetarian and gluten-free offerings, this restaurant invites diners on a journey of culinary exploration and discovery, where every meal is a testament to the chef's dedication to excellence and the joy of sharing good food with good company.
